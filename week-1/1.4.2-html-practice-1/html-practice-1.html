Why I joined Freemote

I've been on the quest to become a full-time developer for some time now. I attended a web dev bootcamp this past year, but still find myself unsure of my ability to be a strong employee. I decided recently to revisit freemote and use this to help reinforce my skillset so I can land my first full-time developer job or who knows, maybe even become a freelancer. I am very curious about the shopify platform, and I am excited to learn more about it and the opportunities it can create.

Why I joined Freemote continued...

What originally got me started on this path was an unquenchable thirst to transition to a remote life so I can move outside of the United States. I come from a military family and served in the military myself, and through those experiences I spent many years of my life living abroad. I miss the adventure that living someplace unfamiliar brought to my life every single day. I miss the great food, the hospitable people, and the complete absence of the weight existing in the western world brings.

I'm trying my best to not dream too hard about where this might take me until it's the right time to do so. When Aaron stated in a previous video that there is "a lot of code between you and that beach in Thailand" I really felt that. My mission right now is to become the most productivite version of myself I have ever been. I know if I can keep after it then this just might be the greatest decision I've ever made.
